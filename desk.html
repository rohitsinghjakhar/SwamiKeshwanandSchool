Courses Offered

Streams Offered at Senior Secondary Level:

  • Science Stream: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics. (Career Focus on Engineering, Medicine, Research, and Technology.)
  • Arts Stream: History, Political Science, Geography, English Literature and many more. (Career Focus: Civil Services, Humanities Research, and Social Work.)

Skill Development Programs:

  • Communication and Soft Skills Training.
  • Public Speaking and Debate Clubs.
  • Leadership and Team-Building Workshops.

Co-Curricular Courses:

  • Performing Arts: Dance, Drama, Music.
  • Fine Arts: Painting, Sculpting, Photography.
  • Sports and Physical Education: Training in athletics, team sports, and yoga.

Competitive Exam Preparation:

  • For Science Students: JEE, NEET, NTSE.
  • For Arts Students: UPSC, SSC, CLAT.

Scholarships and Financial Aid:

  • Special preparatory classes for meritorious students aiming for national and state-level scholarships.
