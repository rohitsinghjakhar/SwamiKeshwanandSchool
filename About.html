Know Our School

Swami Keshwanand Sr. Sec. Shikshan Sansthan, established in 1998 by Mordhwaj Singh, is a prominent educational institution located in Laxmangarh, Alwar, Rajasthan. Affiliated with the Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (BSER), and approved by the Government of Rajasthan, the institution is dedicated to providing quality education that meets both national and international standards.

Vision and Mission

The institution aims to develop moral values in students, integrating traditional and modern educational practices to create well-rounded individuals capable of contributing positively to society. Their mission focuses on implementing result-oriented programs that address global needs, fostering a culture of innovation, inclusivity, and lifelong learning.

Facilities and Programs

Keshwanand Sr. Sec. Shikshan Sansthan offers a range of facilities and programs, including:
  • Library: A well-equipped library that serves as the heart of the academic institution, encouraging students and staff to engage in continuous learning.
  • Laboratories: Modern laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, equipped with necessary tools for practical experiments.
  • Computer Lab: State-of-the-art computer labs with the latest operating systems and hardware, providing students with essential technological skills.
  • Sports Facilities: Emphasis on physical education with facilities for various sports and games, promoting overall well-being.
  • Hostel and Mess: Air-cooled hostels designed to provide a conducive living and learning environment, along with nutritious meals to ensure students' health.
  • Transportation: An excellent fleet of comfortable, well-maintained, air-conditioned buses with trained staff, ensuring safe and convenient travel for students.

Academic Achievements

The institution has a track record of academic excellence, with students achieving top ranks in various examinations. Notably, in the 2024 12th-grade results, Nisha Meena secured 99.20% in the Science stream, and Isha Chaudhary achieved 96.80% in the Arts stream.
