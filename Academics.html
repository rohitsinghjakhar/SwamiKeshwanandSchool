Academic Excellence

Keshwanand Sr. Sec. Shikshan Sansthan offers a range of facilities and programs, including:
  • Academic Philosophy: Our institution is committed to nurturing young minds with a holistic approach to education. We aim to build critical thinking, creativity, and a sense of responsibility, preparing students for a rapidly changing world.

Curriculum Overview

  • Primary Education (Classes 1-5): A strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and moral values through interactive and experiential learning methods.
  • Middle School (Classes 6-8): Advanced subject-specific learning with exposure to co-curricular activities like science fairs, arts, and sports.
  • Secondary Education (Classes 9-10):Focused preparation for board exams with an emphasis on core subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages.
  • Senior Secondary Education (Classes 11-12): Offering specialized streams (Science and Arts) to align with students' career aspirations.
  • Graduation: Offering specialized Bachelor degree with streams (Science and Arts) to align with students' career aspirations with NCC (National Cadet Core).
  • Diploma: Offering BSTC (Diploma in Elementary Education) and B.Ed.Bachelor of Education for Girls.

Facilities for Academic Excellence

  • Library: A strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and moral values through interactive and experiential learning methods.
  • Laboratories: State-of-the-art Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer labs for hands-on experimentation and learning.
  • Smart Classrooms:Technology-enabled classrooms with projectors and interactive whiteboards for engaging lessons.

Academic Achievements

  • Our students have consistently excelled in board exams, securing top ranks and scholarships for higher education.
