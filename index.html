Facilities

Keshwanand Group of Higher Education offers a well-organized transportation system for students and staff. The institution provides buses covering key routes to ensure safe, reliable, and convenient travel for all...

The college has well-maintained hostel facilities for both boys and girls, providing a comfortable and secure environment. The hostels are equipped with essential amenities, including clean rooms, 24/7 water supply, and Wi-Fi, ensuring a conducive atmosphere for studying and living...

A modern and well-equipped computer lab is available with high-speed internet and the latest hardware and software. It provides students with hands-on experience and supports their academic and research needs...

Advanced laboratories for various disciplines are provided to facilitate practical learning and experimentation. These labs are equipped with state-of-the-art instruments and resources to meet academic and research requirements...

The campus has a hygienic and spacious cafeteria that serves nutritious and affordable meals. It offers a variety of food options to cater to the diverse tastes of students and staff...

A wide range of sports facilities is available, including indoor and outdoor games, to promote physical fitness and teamwork. The college organizes regular tournaments and encourages participation at inter-college and state levels...

To nurture creativity and artistic skills, the institution provides dedicated spaces for art and craft activities. Students are encouraged to participate in workshops and competitions to enhance their creative abilities...
